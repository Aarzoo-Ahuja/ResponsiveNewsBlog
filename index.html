Tech Giants Warnings!

Posted on July 6,2021 // Category: Category One

US tech giants Facebook Inc, Alphabet Inc’s Google and Twitter Inc have privately warned the Hong Kong government that they could stop offering their services in the city if authorities proceed with planned changes to data-protection laws, the Wall Street Journal reported on Monday, citing a letter.The laws could make the tech companies liable for the malicious sharing of individuals’ information online, the newspaper added.

Corona Second Wave Still On!

Posted on July 6,2021 // Category: Category Three

The second wave is still existent in a limited way in some areas, said Lav Agarwal, Joint Secretary, Health Ministry on Tuesday. Gross violations of Covid-19 appropriate behaviour can nullify the gains so far, it added. Agarwal said people travelling to hill stations are not following COVID-appropriate behaviour. 73 districts across 17 states, UTs have reported more than 10% Covid positivity rate in June 29-July 5 week.
